Delete your Chargeprice User Account

In order to delete your Chargeprice User Account, you need to perform the following steps, depending on the platform you are using.

Android

  1. Ensure that you are logged in
  2. Go to Profil (Top right icon on Map view)
  3. Overflow Menu (=Three dots on the top right) -> Delete account
  4. Confirm deletion

iOS

  1. Ensure that you are logged in
  2. Go to Settings (Top left icon on Map view) -> My Profile (click on your name) 
  3. Scroll to the button
  4. Click "Delete account" and confirm deletion

Affected Data

The above action will delete the following data, related to your account:

  • Username, E-Mail, Encrypted Password
  • Date of Account Creation
  • Selected Vehicle
  • Selected Tariffs
